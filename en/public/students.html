No Pain No Gain

To be a student at the BI-Fudan MBA program is an inspection of your abilities to manage a hectic day whilst achieving desired results in your professional, personal and academic life.

As a student, you will meet your peers from Thursday to Sunday approximately once every month. It is required that the student are well prepared for the courses and actively participate in classroom discussions and groups works. Some courses obligate the students to conduct pre- and post-course assignments. However, the preparation for each class seldom start earlier than two weeks prior to the class.

We strive for our students to have a life long experience that creates memories of overcoming barriers, developing a new mind-set, but also enjoyment, amusement and fun. You will get new friends that often lasts for a lifetime, and we foster such environment by organizing social events all year long
